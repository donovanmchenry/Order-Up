How To Play

1. Click "Start Game" to begin.
2. Memorize the ingredients displayed on the cards, they correspond to the dish.
3. When the cards flip back over, you must click the ingredients.
4. If you get it right, you'll serve the dish! If not, the game will end.
5. Try to serve as many dishes as possible to beat your high score!
